Conference Structure MOSC MY

Mon, Feb 13, 2017

MOSCMY Conference Structure 1. Talk and Tutorials: There will be 24 talks and tutorial sessions 2. Competition : There will be programming competitions for Enterprise Open Source solution. 3. Demonstration Booth : There will be 20 booths for sponsors to exhibit and demonstrate their products, services and projects in detail to participants. 4. Birds of a Feather : There will be Birds of a Feather sessions, where participants with the same interest can get together to have a lively dialogue about their Open Source project.
